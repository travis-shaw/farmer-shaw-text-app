SMS Opt-In

Farmer Shaw is a conversational garden assistant operated by Travis Shaw and reached by text message.

How Users Opt In

End users opt in by texting START, YES, or UNSTOP to the Farmer Shaw phone number at +1 350-220-4744. This is the SMS keyword opt-in path for Farmer Shaw.

Confirmation Message

Farmer Shaw: You are now opted in to receive conversational garden help, plant photo replies, and reminders you request. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help or STOP to opt out.

Help Message

Farmer Shaw: For help, text your garden or plant question and we will reply. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out.

Opt-Out Message

Farmer Shaw: You have opted out and will no longer receive messages. Reply START to opt back in.

What Users Receive

Users receive conversational replies to plant and gardening questions, responses to plant photos they send, saved garden context, and reminders they specifically request.

Required Disclosures

  • Message frequency varies.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP to opt out.
  • Reply HELP for help.
