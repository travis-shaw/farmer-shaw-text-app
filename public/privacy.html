Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Farmer Shaw is a text-message garden assistant operated by Travis Shaw. This policy explains what information is collected when you text Farmer Shaw and how that information is used.

Information We Collect

We may collect your mobile phone number, text messages, photos you send, approximate location information you provide, garden notes, plantings, reminders, and related conversation history.

How We Use Information

We use this information to respond to gardening questions, analyze plant photos, remember garden context, create user-requested reminders, improve service quality, prevent abuse, and comply with legal or carrier requirements.

Text Messaging Program

By texting START, YES, or UNSTOP to Farmer Shaw at +1 350-220-4744, you agree to receive conversational SMS/MMS garden help, plant photo replies, and reminders you request from Farmer Shaw. Message frequency varies based on your conversations and reminders you request. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.

SMS Opt-In

Users opt in by texting START, YES, or UNSTOP to the Farmer Shaw phone number at +1 350-220-4744. The opt-in flow is described at https://mortician-favoring-smudgy.ngrok-free.dev/sms.

Mobile Information and Messaging Consent

We do not share, sell, rent, or otherwise provide your mobile phone number, mobile information, opt-in data, or messaging consent information to any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Service Providers

We may use service providers to operate Farmer Shaw, including messaging, AI, hosting, and storage providers. These providers process information only as needed to provide Farmer Shaw. They are not allowed to use mobile information or messaging consent information for their own marketing or promotional purposes.

Messaging Use

Farmer Shaw sends only conversational garden assistance, plant photo replies, and user-requested reminders. Farmer Shaw does not send marketing or promotional messages for other businesses.

Photos and Garden Content

If you send plant photos or garden details, those messages may be processed to generate advice. Do not send sensitive personal information through text message.

Retention

We keep conversation and reminder information as long as reasonably needed to provide the service, maintain garden memory, comply with obligations, resolve disputes, and improve reliability.

Your Choices

You may reply STOP to opt out of text messages. You may request help by replying HELP. You may also ask us to delete garden memory associated with your phone number.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Farmer Shaw operator through the phone number where you receive Farmer Shaw messages.
